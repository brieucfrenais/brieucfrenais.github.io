I have been a PhD student at Université de Strasbourg since September 2021, under the supervision of Jean Bérard and Lionel Lenôtre. We study branching processes with selection such as N-Branching Brownian motions, and their links with free boundary problems. Here is my resume.

In 2024-2025, I have a non-tenured teaching and research position (ATER) at the Ecole des Mines de Nancy and the Institut Elie Cartan. The classes I give are detailed on the teaching page (French version only). I was also co-organizer of the IRMA PhD students' seminar with Thomas Saigre during my second year.
